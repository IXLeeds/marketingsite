Board

IXLeeds is a limited company with a board of four directors and a company secretary.

IXLeeds' first board was elected on 21st September 2011 by its founding members and comprises:

Chair

Andy Davidson, Asteroid International

Andy also sits on the board of the European Internet Exchange Association (Euro-IX), the trade body for exchange points, and is the Chief Technology Officer of Asteroid International BV, a company who develop software to run automated, reliable Internet Exchange Points. Previously he was a Director of LONAP (another independent IXP based in the UK), a council member of NAPAfrica (the largest IXP in the continent of Africa), and the CTO of Allegro Networks (the world's first entirely automated wholesale carrier network).
Finance Director

Mark Fordyce, York Data Services

 Mark is the founder and Managing Director of York Data Services Limited, a business ISP based in York serving over 200 hundred businesses primarily across North Yorkshire. He is also sector chair for Science City York's digital industries and part of the Broadband Task Group for the York & North Yorkshire LEP. Mark has also been an advisor to Yorkshire Forward as a member of the CDI advisory board over the past ten years.
Director

Tom Bird, Portfast

 Tom works with a number of organisations including LONAP and the BBC, where he is involved in several technical projects. In addition to this, he is the founder of Portfast Ltd, a small service provider that peers in the UK.
Technical Director

Thomas Mangin, Exa Networks

 Thomas is a founder and Technical Director for Exa Networks Limited, a business and education ISP, and currently serves as a non-executive director at the London Internet Exchange (LINX). Thomas has been involved with mutual organisations and exchanges for over 10 years.
Company Secretary

Adam Beaumont, AQL

 Adam is the founder and Managing Director of aql, a specialist communications company whose datacentre houses IXLeeds. His current involvements are a WiFi innovation company, a GSM engineering company, a cafe-bar and a whitelabel wholesale telecommunications operator.
