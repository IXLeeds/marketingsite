IXLeeds 2

IXLeeds 2 takes place at the Carriageworks, Leeds on 12th September 2012.

IXLeeds 2 is our second event, open to members and non-members. This is your chance to find out more about the organisation, hear some interesting related speakers and network with other attendees.

The event is aimed at our members and supporters as well as any organisations currently considering membership or joining our community.

It will also be of interest to organisations who understand the importance of the Internet to their business and who would like to know more about how we are working towards ensuring a more efficient Internet for end users.

The programme was put together by our board, if you would like to suggest speakers for our next event please let us know.
