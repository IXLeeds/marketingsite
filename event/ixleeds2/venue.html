IXLeeds 2

IXLeeds 2 takes place at the Carriageworks, Leeds on 12th September 2012.

The Carriageworks Theatre is a short walk from Leeds Train Station and overlooks Millennium Square.

The adress for the venue is The Electric Press, 3 Millennium Square, Leeds, LS2 3AD.

More information on the location and nearby parking facilities can be found at the Carriageworks website.

The evening social will take place in All Bar One, on the ground floor of the Carriageworks.
