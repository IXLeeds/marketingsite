IXLeeds 4

IXLeeds 4 will take place at aql's Salem Chapel, Leeds on 4th December 2014.



The aql conference centre is located within aql's headquarters in Salem Church, which is a 10 minute walk from the Leeds Train station and only a short drive from the M621.

The adress for the venue is:
AQL, Salem Church, Hunslet Road, Leeds, LS10 1JW.



By rail

Taxis are available at the main entrance, or you can walk to our offices in ten minutes.

Walk along Boar Lane until you reach the crossroads with Briggate. Turn right and continue under the railway bridge, over the river Aire, past the Adelphi and look for Salem Church before the entrance to Tetley's Brewery.


Driving directions

At J43 of the M1, join the M621 towards Leeds (centre), Leave the M621 at junction M621 J3-J4 and straight ahead into A653 (Dewsbury Road) direction Holbeck, Central Leeds. Bear right onto Great Wilson Street. Continue straight onto Meadow Lane. Continue straight onto Hunslet Road where you will see the aql building by Tetley's brewery.

Some Sat Nav systems have difficulty locating Salem Church by postcode. We therefore recommend using the adjacent street: Dock Street, Leeds, LS10 1LX. Salem Church is situated 100 yards from the Adelphi pub, which is on the corner of Hunslet road and Dock Street.
