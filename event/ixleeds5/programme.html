IXLeeds 5

IXLeeds 5 will take place at aql's Salem Chapel, Leeds on 18th October 2016.

Speaker Programme

12:00 - Register / Coffee and Tea

13:30 - Welcome and thanks to sponsors

13:40 : First session

  • - Welcome to venue - Adam Beaumont

  • - IXLeeds Update - Andy Davidson

  • - Euro-IX Update - Andy Davidson

  • - IXLeeds Technical Update/Cumulus - Tom Bird

  • - Connection Vouchers / Open Market Review - Mark Durham, Superfast Cities, Leeds City Council

14:50 - Break / Coffee and Tea / Networking

15:10 - Big Data meets Big Traffic

  • - Leeds Data Mill Intro - Leanne Buchan, LDM

  • - Data, Uses and Issues - Brian Ablett, Leeds City Council Smart Cities

  • - Smart Cities - What's the Point? - Ian Jones, Leeds City Council Smart Cities

  • - The ESRC Consumer Data Research Centre at Leeds - Martin Clarke, University of Leeds

  • - Cloud Data Analytics: Next Generation Big Data and Cloud Computing Research in Datacenters - Dr Peter Garraghan, University of Leeds School of Computing

17:00 - ISP Topics

  • - CPE Evolution - Anthony Magee, Adva

  • - IPv6 in Data Centres - Tom Hill, Bytemark Hosting

18:00 - Social!



Education Programme

IXLeeds directors will also provide information about BGP, peering and internet services in Yorkshire to anyone curious about Internet Exchange Points and how to benefit from peering.

Interested participants should declare their interest at registration.
