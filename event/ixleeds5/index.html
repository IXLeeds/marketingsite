IXLeeds 5

IXLeeds 5 will take place at aql's Salem Chapel, Leeds on 18 October 2016.

IXLeeds 5 is open to members and non-members. This is your chance to find out more about the organisation, hear some interesting related speakers and network with other attendees. It will also be of interest to organisations who understand the importance of the Internet to their business and who would like to know more about how we are working towards ensuring a more efficient Internet for end users.

The programme is being put together by our board, if you would like to suggest speakers please let us know.
